- A clear fault line exists between AI optimists (AlexFinn) who see limitless democratisation and AI skeptics (atmoio, fchollet) who see inflated terminology and misinformation

- The discourse reflects a broader tension: practitioners shipping real tools with AI vs. commentators debating whether the underlying technology deserves its labels

## TLDR

The AI community is split: builders like steipete are shipping tools that close tens of thousands of GitHub issues with AI assistance, while researchers like fchollet and atmoio are pushing back hard on inflated terminology and influencer misinformation. AlexFinn sits firmly in the optimist camp, calling AI the most democratising technology ever and praising Codex as a Ferrari for developers.
